    I am a third-year Electrical Engineering student currently studying at McMaster University. From a young age, I have always had a competitive spirit and an unrelenting passion for problem-solving. Whether it was cracking riddles or solving a Rubik's Cube, I found joy in the process and a deep sense of accomplishment in overcoming challenges. Realizing this desire, I chose to study engineering—a field that perfectly aligns with my love for innovation and discovery.
